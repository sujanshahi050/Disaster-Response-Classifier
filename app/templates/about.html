About

Overview

This is a web application that classifies a text into different categories. It simulates a real world situation where during a natural calmity or a disaster, tweets from people can be collected , classified and analyzed in order to send out appropriate help to those in need. The categories of help range from rescuing people to supplying water.

How it works?

On the top left hand corner, you can see an option Home. If you click on it, it will take you to the homepage. It is self explanatory and easy to use.
